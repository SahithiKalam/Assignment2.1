Nani

Ghanta Naveen Babu, known professionally by his screen name Nani, is an Indian actor, film producer and television presenter who predominantly works in Telugu cinema. Nani was born on 24 February 1984 in a Hindu-Telugu family and was brought up in Hyderabad. His family hails from Challapalli, Andhra Pradesh. Nani did his schooling from St. Alphonsa's High School, Hyderabad, and then did his intermediate from Narayana Junior College, S. R. Nagar before graduating from Wesley Degree College, Secunderabad. After five years of courtship, Nani married Anjana Yelavarthy on 27 October 2012. Their son Arjun, was born in 2017.
